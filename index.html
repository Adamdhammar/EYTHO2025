1. English Competition

Essay Poster Competition

The Essay Competition invites participants to share innovative ideas on the challenges and opportunities in Bali's hospitality industry. It enhances writing, critical thinking, and idea presentation skills. Judging criteria include originality, depth of analysis, and writing quality. Join us and contribute to the future of Bali tourism!
